Port Hills

This is a Volunteer program aimed at the restoration and maintenance of the native forest area of Port Hills. Volunteers will be planting trees each winter and maintaining them in spring and autumn. Come prepared for all weather and wear sturdy footwear for hill work. A limited number of hedge clippers and gloves will be provided by the rangers, but feel free to bring your own.

Eskdale Reserve

This is a Volunteer program led by expert ecologists at Eskdale Reserve, one of Auckland’s largest Significant Ecological Areas, dedicated to the preservation of this special reserve and making a positive impact on the environment. Come and join “behind-the-scenes” and see what it takes to keep our local nature reserves thriving! Cost is free.
